Living with your sister x action battle x roguelike x open world fantasy RPG game!<br>

In order to cure his sister's special constitution,<br>
he constantly searches for materials to make the all-purpose elixir "red cerebrospinal fluid" in the Abyss.<br>
If he cannot complete it within the time limit, his sister...!
